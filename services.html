Home Services

1. Comprehensive 24/7 Emergency Medical Coverage

Continuous, round-the-clock access to medical care

  • 24/7 On-Call Medical Support: Emergency response available at any time, no matter when or where you need it.
  • Unlimited On-Site Emergency Response: Anytime a medical emergency occurs at your organization, a trained medical team is dispatched immediately.
  • Health Risk Mitigation & Proactive Care: Monthly health check-ins to assess potential risks and ensure that the environment is as safe as possible.
  • Global Coverage: If an emergency occurs at an event or while traveling, medical support is available, no matter the location.

2. Corporate & Event-Wide Medical Coverage

Large organizations or those frequently hosting events

  • Constant On-Site Presence: Medical teams are available to be stationed on-site as needed, whether at offices, gyms, or event venues.
  • On-Demand Medical Support for Special Events: Coverage for any unplanned or emergency events, such as accidents or injuries at large-scale gatherings.
  • Annual Health & Safety Audit: In-depth review of your organization's health protocols, emergency response times, and risk assessment.
  • Priority Medical Response: Guaranteed arrival times, regardless of the situation. This provides peace of mind that if something happens, help is already on its way.

3. Elite Family & Organization Medical Subscription

Comprehensive, all-encompassing coverage for organizations, families, and frequent travelers

  • 24/7 Medical Coverage for Employees & Families: A flexible, comprehensive plan that covers not just the organization but also employees’ families, ensuring they're covered wherever they are.
  • Dedicated Personal Medical Team: A dedicated team that provides personalized care and attention, from health monitoring to immediate medical intervention.
  • Annual Physical & Wellness Checks: Routine medical check-ups and wellness programs to maintain overall health within the organization.
  • Unlimited Emergency Medical Response: Whether it’s a work injury, home emergency, or travel-related accident, you are covered without any additional fees.
  • Mental Health Support: In addition to physical health care, this plan provides ongoing mental health support, including counseling and crisis intervention.
